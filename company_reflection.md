# Company Reflection

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
The founder couldn't find any apps that were useful, so he made one for ADHD/neurodivergent people. 
It's meant to help neurodivergent people focus by removing distractions, motivate you to start the 
day right, and use more visuals to help you focus.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Helping neurodivergent people be productive who cant focus due to the increased ditractions of modern technology.

## Why do you think this mission is important?
In today's age it's very hard to concentrate. Our attention span is decreasing, and even a person 
like me who doesn't have ADHD struggles to focus for longer hours.

## How does Focus Bear's work align with your personal values or interests?
I do believe some of us are more at an advantage than people who find it difficult to focus through 
no fault of their own. This app is a step toward bringing everyone to the same level, and as a whole 
it just allows people to form better habits and improve their quality of life, which I find really 
nice personally.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes, I do find it hard to concentrate even though I haven't been diagnosed with anything.

## Do you know someone who has struggled, and how might Focus Bear help them?
Yes, my cousin is actually dyslexic and goes to a different school. An app like this can actually 
help him focus, and even me for that matter. Even someone who doesn't struggle with it should use 
a focus app, as it might just enhance their focus.
