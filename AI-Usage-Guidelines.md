# AI Usage Guidelines

## Research & Learn

### What AI tools are typically used for your role?
As a full stack intern, AI tools can help with things like writing and debugging code, drafting 
documentation, summarising information, and speeding up repetitive tasks like writing 
boilerplate code or comments.

### What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting?
Benefits include saving time, speeding up repetitive work, and helping explain unfamiliar 
concepts. AI is efficient and can help refine work, but the actual ideas should still come from 
our own thinking. Risks include AI giving incorrect or outdated information, over relying on it 
instead of understanding the work yourself, and accidentally sharing private or confidential 
information with an AI tool.

### What types of information should never be entered into AI tools?
Passwords, API keys, tokens, personal identifying information, confidential company data, and 
any sensitive user or personal data should never be entered into AI tools, especially ones 
outside company approved systems.

### How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy?
By cross checking the information with official documentation, testing code before trusting it, 
searching for a second source to confirm facts, and not assuming AI output is correct just 
because it sounds confident.

## Reflection

### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?
AI is useful for speeding up small tasks, getting unstuck, or explaining something unfamiliar. 
But for important decisions, actual understanding of the code, or anything sensitive, I should 
rely on my own skills and double check things myself instead of blindly trusting AI output.

### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?
By treating AI as a helper rather than the final answer, always reviewing and understanding what 
it gives me before using it, and making sure I can explain or justify anything I use, rather than 
copying it without understanding it.

### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?
I will avoid entering any passwords, tokens, confidential company information, or personal user 
data into AI tools. I will also use only approved tools where possible and be mindful of what 
information I share.
