# Competitive Landscape

## What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?
Most other apps only do one thing well. Cold Turkey and Freedom are mainly blockers, while 
Tiimo and Routinery are mainly planners. Focus Bear tries to combine blocking distractions 
and building routines in one app, along with being designed for neurodivergent users.

## If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?
Cold Turkey is not free and has some sketchy signs, like reviews mentioning spelling errors 
in the app and people saying it was hard to cancel the trial or delete the app. Routinery users 
also say the free version has too many ads, and it's only good if you pay. Focus Bear avoids 
these issues while still combining the features people want.

## What's one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn't?
Tiimo has an easier and more visual interface, plus an AI brain dump feature where you can just 
type or speak what you need to do and it automatically turns it into organised tasks. Tiimo also 
works well across devices, including the Apple Watch. Focus Bear does not have this kind of AI 
brain dump feature or as smooth a visual planning experience.

## Based on your research, what's one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?
Focus Bear could add something similar to Tiimo's AI brain dump feature, where users can quickly 
type or speak their tasks and have them automatically organised, instead of manually entering 
everything.
