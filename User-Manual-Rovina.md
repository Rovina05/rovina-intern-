# User Manual for Rovina

## My role
I am working as a Full Stack Intern on the SecureShift capstone project and completing Focus Bear 
onboarding tasks.
The days and times I work are Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, generally between 10am 
and 4pm, with my exact hours shifting a little depending on the week.

## How I Work Best
I do my best work when I have a set schedule and know what is expected of me in advance.
I struggle when I am put on the spot with sudden questions, as it can make me anxious.

## Communication Style
I prefer messages over Discord text, but I find it easier to ask questions or clear doubts during 
stand up meetings.
The best way to reach me is Discord.
If you need something urgent, message me directly on Discord and I will respond as soon as I can.

## Collaboration & Teamwork
I work best in a team when I feel prepared and know what's coming, rather than being asked sudden 
questions on the spot, since that can make me anxious.
My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is being put on the spot without any warning or context.
If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means I am taking time to process, not that I am 
disengaged.

## Focus & Productivity
I stay focused by keeping my phone away and working with a strict deadline.
Things that distract me include having my phone nearby.
The best way to support my productivity is giving me clear deadlines and letting me work with 
minimal interruptions.

## Feedback & Growth
I like feedback to be given in a nice, supportive manner.
When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is kindly and directly, without making me feel 
anxious about it.
I appreciate it when teammates communicate feedback gently and clearly.

## Fun Facts & Quirks
One thing people often misunderstand about me is that I'm quiet, when actually I just take time to 
warm up to people. Once I'm comfortable, I'm quite chatty.
A fun fact about me is that I like to ask a lot of questions and get to know people properly.
My work superpower is being able to connect with people once I'm comfortable, and asking good 
questions to really understand a task or a person.
